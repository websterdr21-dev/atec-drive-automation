### Human Verification Required

#### 1. Direct ATEC bookout — site-root unit folder auto-creation

**Test:** Start a fresh `/bookout` session in Telegram. Paste a ticket for a Direct ATEC site. When the guided navigation prompt appears, press `u` immediately without entering a subfolder number.
**Expected:** Bot creates `Unit [N]` under `Sites/[site_name]/`, replies with "Unit N (auto-created)" in the confirmation, uploads the serial-number photo to the new unit folder, and saves the template so a second bookout for the same site skips the navigation prompt entirely.
**Why human:** Requires a live Telegram bot instance and a real (or test-environment) Google Drive to verify the folder is actually created and photos land in it.

#### 2. Serial correction — corrected serial found in stock

**Test:** Trigger a bookout where the OCR serial is invalid (not in any stock sheet). When the bot sends the correction prompt with the serial in backticks, reply with the correct serial number.
**Expected:** Bot replies "Found `CORRECTED_SERIAL` in stock sheets.", then proceeds with stock-sheet update (row goes red) and outputs the accounts email.
**Why human:** Requires live Telegram session and real stock sheets accessible to the service account.

#### 3. Serial correction — user replies 'swap'

**Test:** At the serial correction prompt, reply with `swap` (case-insensitive).
**Expected:** Bot skips the stock-sheet update, skips the accounts email, and proceeds directly to folder creation and photo upload (swap mode).
**Why human:** Requires live Telegram session; FSM transition and message flow cannot be tested offline without a full bot harness.

#### 4. Serial correction — corrected serial still not found

**Test:** At the serial correction prompt, reply with another serial number that is also not in any stock sheet.
**Expected:** Bot sends the swap-warning message and transitions to STEP_SWAP_CONFIRM, offering to continue as a swap.
**Why human:** Requires live Telegram session with a controlled stock sheet returning no matches for the corrected input.
